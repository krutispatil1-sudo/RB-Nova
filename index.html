Led by civil engineer Rathod Bhimsen, RB Nova brings 4 years of detail-oriented engineering, site execution expertise, and thoughtful design coordination to residential and commercial developments.

Our approach is grounded in being present on site, understanding the drawings deeply, and making sure each decision serves both the strength of the structure and the comfort of the people who will live or work inside it.

With hands-on experience across apartment, condominium, and residential projects, RB Nova translates architectural and structural drawings into precise on-site execution, supported by estimation, planning, quality control, and close coordination with contractors, consultants, and vendors.

About The Engineer

Rathod Bhimsen
Civil Engineer & Site Designer

Rathod Bhimsen is a detail-oriented civil engineer experienced in site execution, structural design coordination, estimation, and project planning for residential and commercial developments.

He translates architectural, structural, and RCC drawings into accurate on-site execution while coordinating with contractors, architects, consultants, and vendors to maintain quality, cost control, safety, and timelines.
